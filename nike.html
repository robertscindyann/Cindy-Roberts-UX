This project challenged me to design a Nike ad concept that captures the brand’s iconic tone of motivation, movement, and bold simplicity. My goal was to interpret Nike’s design language while adding my own creative touch.

Research & Influence

Before designing, I collected images from Nike campaigns and other visuals that inspired me. These references helped me analyze Nike’s use of bold imagery, typography, and messaging style. They guided my approach while ensuring my ad felt authentic to the brand.

Final Design: Ignite Your Run

This ad was created to capture the energy and excitement of running. Using Photoshop, I combined custom imagery—including a sneaker and flame effects— to create a bold, high-impact visual.
