Wildcats West Johnston High School Dance Team Logo

I designed a custom logo for the Wildcats Dance Team at West Johnston High School. The goal was to create a strong, unified identity that could be featured across apparel and team branding. The logo was approved by the coach and principal and is proudly worn by the team on t-shirts and used in group photos.

Skills Used

  • Logo Design
  • Brand Identity
  • Illustrator

Project Impact

The logo not only elevated the team’s visual identity but also built team spirit. It demonstrated my ability to design professional branding elements that resonate with both students and leadership.
