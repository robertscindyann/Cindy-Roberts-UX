Diabetes Awareness Website

This project involved designing and coding a responsive health awareness website aimed at providing accessible resources and information for users managing diabetes.

Target Audience

This website was specifically designed for high school students learning about diabetes. The content was simplified into accessible, engaging sections with visuals that make health information less intimidating. The goal was to empower students with knowledge they can easily understand and apply in daily life.

Project Goals

  • Provide clear, accessible information for users with diabetes
  • Create a visually appealing and easy-to-navigate layout
  • Showcase both design and frontend coding skills

UX Research & Planning

To guide content and feature decisions, I created a hub-and-spoke diagram outlining the key topics and user pathways.
